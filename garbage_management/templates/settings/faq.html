Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Garbage Management System?

It's a platform to monitor, manage, and optimize the garbage collection and disposal process efficiently using modern technology.

Who can use this system?

Municipal authorities, private waste management companies, and community administrators can benefit from this system.

How do I report a complaint?

You can use the "Complaint Register" link in the sidebar after logging in. Your complaint will be addressed by the concerned authority.

How can I change my account details?

Go to the "Settings" page and view your profile section. Editing options will be available if permitted for your role.
